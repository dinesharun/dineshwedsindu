Tourist Places In and Around Tirunelveli

When you make a trip to tirunelveli, there are several things to do and see. Among numerous choices there are some popular and well accessible places that are easy to visit.
  • Thenmalai
  • Manjoolai
  • Koonthankulam
  • Nellaiappar & Kanthimahi Amman Temple
  • Courtallam
  • Papanasam
  • KanyaKumari
Choose anyone of these from the banners above to know detailed info.
Thenmalai

Located 45 kms from Courtallam, Thenmala is a small village at the foothills of Western Ghats and predominantly a forest area. The famous Shenduruney Wildlife Sanctuary forms the main attraction of Thenmala Ecotourism.

36 km from from Tenkasi, Bus facilities available from Tenkasi
Manjoolai

A perfect summer resort set deep in the Western ghats is blessed with a scenic beauty and peaceful environment.

Located between elevations ranging from 1000 to 1500 Metres, the Manjolai area is set deep within the Western Ghats within the Kalakad Mundanthurai Tiger Reserve in the Tirunelveli District. Located on top of the Manimuthar Dam & the Manimuthar Water Falls, the Manjolai area comprises Tea Plantations, Small settlements around the tea plantations; Upper Kodaiyar Dam and a windy view point called Kuthiravetti

57 km from from Tenaksi and 63 km from Tirunelveli, Bus facilities available from Tirunelveli
Koonthankulam

It is just a short distance away from Tirunelveli having more than 43 species of resident and migratory water birds visit here every year and more than 100,000 migratory birds start coming by December and fly away to their northern homes by June or July after they lay and hatch eggs and the young ones mature enough to fly with the older ones.

20 km from from Tirunelveli, Bus facilities available from Tirunelveli
Nellaiappar & Kanthimahi Amman Temple

The temple of Nellaiappar and Kanthimathi is situated in the centre of the town. The very name of the town, Tirunelveli which was known in the past as Then Pandyanagaram is a part of the town which has developed around the temple of Nellaiyappar. From a distance itself one can have a beautiful view of the great gopuram tower. Both the gopurams were built according to the rules laid down in the agamasastras by Rama Pandyan.

In the heart of Tirunelveli, Several Bus facilities available
Courtallam
Located 14 kms from pavoorchatram Courtallam is called "spa of the south" which is an excellent health resort and famous for the waterfalls.

Places to visit in Courtallam:
  • Peraruvi(Main Falls),
  • Chitraruvi(Small Falls),
  • Shenbaga devi Falls,
  • Thenaruvi(Honey Falls),
  • Anitharuvi(Five Falls),
  • Pazhathotta Aruvi,
  • puli Aruvi(tiger Falls),
  • Pazhaya Courtallam(old Falls)
  • ShenbagaDevi Amman Temple
  • ChithiraSabai

Just 6 km from from Tenaksi, Several Bus facilities available from Tenkasi
Papanasam
Is a famous picnic spot located 60 kms from Tirunelveli. The famous Shiva temple is located at the bottom of Western Ghats and also very near to the origin of the River thamirabarani.

Places to visit in Papanasam:
  • Papanasam River
  • Agasthiar Falls
  • Vaana Theertham Falls
  • Papanasam dam
  • Papanasam Kaarayaar Dam
  • Servalar Dam
  • Manimutthaar Dam
  • Baana Theertham falls
  • Kalakkad Mundanthurai Tiger Reserve

34 km from from Tenkasi, Several Bus facilities available from Tenaksi
KanyaKumari
Kanyakumari town has tourist attractions of its own, the district has many more, from centuries-old historic and religious sites to scenic places.A unique feature of Kanyakumari district is its diversity of natural ecosystems, including beaches, mountain valleys, evergreen forests in the deep interior, rubber and clove plantations on the highlands located 84 kms from Tirunelveli
Places to visit near KanyaKumari:

  • Thirparapu falls
  • Thotti paalam(Mathur Hanging Trough)
  • Sussendram Temple
  • Vivekananda rock and Thiruvalluvar statue
  • Bhagavathi Amman Temple
  • vattakottai fort
  • Udhayagiri fort
  • Pathmanathapuram palace
  • Kovalam

125 km from from Tirunelveli, Several Bus facilities available from Tirunelveli
